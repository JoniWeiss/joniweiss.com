About Joni

Joni is on a quest to make the world around her a better place for everyone, one application, one website, one organization at a time. She strives to create or enhance user experience with every site and application she touches.

Since completing the 12-week intensive coding bootcamp at The Iron Yard in downtown Salt Lake City, she has been busy working on projects (see below), continuing to learn by coding, code pairing with others in the local JavaScript community.

Additionally, Joni is a regular volunteer with some amazing local meetups dedicated to supporting the development community. Notable among them are UtahJS, Girl Develop It SLC, Free Code Camp SLC Meetup, and SLC JS Learners.

To support herself until she lands a developer role — Joni work weekends providing Massage Therapy for those with sore backs, necks, and shoulders.

Joni looks forward to the day (hopefully very soon) where she'll be shipping code as a paid professional :)

In her spare time, Joni enjoys coffee with friends, volunteering, meditation, cooking, movies, travel, walks in the city, in the park, and — in warmer weather — in our spectacular canyons!
